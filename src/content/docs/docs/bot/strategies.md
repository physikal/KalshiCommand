## Strategy Overview

Document your trading bot's strategies here as you develop them. This page serves as a living reference for the approaches your bot uses.

## Adding Strategies

Update this page with details about each strategy:

- **Name** - A descriptive identifier
- **Market Types** - Which markets the strategy targets
- **Entry Criteria** - When to enter a position
- **Exit Criteria** - When to close or let settle
- **Risk Parameters** - Position sizing and exposure limits
